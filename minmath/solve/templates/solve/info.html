problem generation

Problems are generated according to their operations and operand value ranges. Subtraction and division will reverse the ranges to ensure that answers are nonnegative integers.

There are no division problems that contain zero to avoid division-by-zero errors. In this event, a random mode is selected and replaced for that problem.
